About the Artist

Transforming Visions into Visual Experiences

Elnaz Ashrafi is a contemporary artist whose work explores the intersection of memory, emotion, and abstract expression. Her unique approach combines traditional techniques with modern sensibilities.

With exhibitions across international galleries and a growing collection of commissioned works, Elnaz continues to push the boundaries of contemporary art, creating pieces that resonate with collectors and art enthusiasts worldwide.
