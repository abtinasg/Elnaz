Elnaz Ashrafi is a contemporary Iranian artist whose work explores the delicate intersection of memory, identity, and color.

Based between Tehran and Berlin, her practice bridges Eastern and Western aesthetic traditions, transforming personal and cultural memory into visceral visual experiences.
